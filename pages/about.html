About UniLib

A Modern Library Management System for Students and Libraries

Who We Are

UniLib is a web-based Library Management System designed to simplify library operations and improve access to educational resources. It enables students to search books, view availability, and manage library services through a user-friendly platform.

The system helps librarians manage books, members, and issue/return records efficiently while reducing manual paperwork and improving data accuracy.

🎯 Mission

To provide a centralized digital platform that makes accessing and managing library resources easy, fast, and efficient.

🚀 Vision

To transform traditional libraries into smart digital learning environments that support students and educators.
